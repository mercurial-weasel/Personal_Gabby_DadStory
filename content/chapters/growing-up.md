High school was a blur of bad haircuts and even worse fashion choices. I rode my red Schwinn bicycle everywhere—it was my first taste of true freedom. 

I remember the first time I went to a concert; the music was so loud my chest vibrated. It was then I realized that life wasn't just about following the rules—it was about finding the rhythm that made your heart beat faster.
